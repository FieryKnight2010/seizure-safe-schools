# Quiz: The Minnesota Law

??? quiz "1. What is the correct citation for Minnesota's seizure-safety law?"
    **Minnesota Statutes section 121A.24.** It was enacted in 2021 and applies from the
    2022-23 school year. ("HF 3234, May 2022" is a common incorrect citation.)

??? quiz "2. Which schools does the law cover?"
    All Minnesota **public school districts and charter schools**.

??? quiz "3. What triggers the requirement for a seizure action plan?"
    A parent/guardian notifying the school that the child has a provider-diagnosed seizure
    disorder **and** has prescribed seizure rescue/treatment medication.

??? quiz "4. Name two of the four things a seizure action plan must do."
    Any two: name a trained person at each site; require that person to be trained; provide the
    plan to that person; file it with the principal or nurse (plus notify staff/volunteers).

??? quiz "5. Does the law set a fixed number of required training hours?"
    **No.** It requires that self-study materials be provided. The floor is modest, which is
    why adoption and follow-through (not just the law) matter.
